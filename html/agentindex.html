Orchid Project

The aim of the game is to rescue the injured people, arrest the criminals and put out the fires before time runs out.

To send the emergency services to an incident, first click on an emergency service shield and then click on one of the relevant incident icons

Firefighters can be sent to put out fires
Police can be sent after criminals
Ambulances can be sent to injured people

Enter your details below and click submit to submit your score to the leader board. If you are not at the British Science Festival on a school trip, you can enter your email address in the school box.
